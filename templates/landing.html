What is CST?

Craniosacral Therapy (CST) is a gentle, non-invasive hands-on therapy that focuses on the craniosacral system, which includes the membranes and cerebrospinal fluid that surround the brain and spinal cord. CST helps release tension, improve bodily function, and promote natural healing by facilitating the body's own healing processes. It is widely used to address stress, chronic pain, and various physical and emotional conditions.
